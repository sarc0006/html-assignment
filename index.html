About Me

My name is Claire Sarcia (nicknamed: Al), an aspiring Media and Production director. I was born on the 17th of June in Manila, Philippines. I moved to Oman when I was 3, where I spent most of my childhood growing up. I am the eldest amongst my siblings. I am fond of music like OPM and a few Indie Music.I like birds as pets more than dogs and some cats.

I am passionate about arts, design, and its various forms. I find it very interesting and something worth appreciating. For as long as I can remember, art has always been an integral part of my life. It has brought me comfort and joy whenever and wherever I am. Animated shows like JJK, One Piece, Demon Slayer, Ghibli Movies, etc. are some of the few shows that I enjoy watching.
