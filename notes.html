HTML Tags I have learned and their description:

<h1>
This is the heading tag. <h1> is the most important heading, followed by <h2>, <h3>, and so on.
<p>
This is the paragraph tag.
<em>
This is the emphasis tag. It italicizes text. Screen readers change its voice for this text.
<strong>
This is the strong tag. It makes text bolded. Like the <em> tag, screen readers change its voice for this.
<abbr>
This is the abbreviation tag. It contains the meaning of the acronym and is seen when the cursor hovers over the text.
